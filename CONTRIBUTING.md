# Contributing to Awesome One Person Company

Thanks for helping make this the best resource for solo founders. A few guidelines:

## What belongs here

Resources that directly help someone **build and run a profitable one-person company** — communities, blogs, books, podcasts, tools, AI workflows, templates, and case studies. Quality over quantity.

## How to add a resource

1. Fork this repository.
2. Add your entry to the most relevant section, keeping the existing format:
   ```markdown
   - **[Resource name](https://link)** — One clear sentence describing what it is and why it's useful.
   ```
3. Keep the list alphabetical-ish within a section where it makes sense, and avoid duplicates.
4. Open a pull request with a short note on why the resource is valuable.

## Quality bar

- The resource must be real, live, and genuinely useful.
- Descriptions must be accurate — no marketing fluff.
- No low-quality affiliate-spam or dead links.
- Self-submissions are welcome if the resource clears the same quality bar as everything else.
