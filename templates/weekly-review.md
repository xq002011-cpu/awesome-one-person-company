# Weekly Review Template — One Person Company

> A 30-minute weekly operating cadence to stay on top of metrics, pipeline, and priorities when you are the entire team. Copy this into [Notion](https://www.notion.so/) and run it every Friday (or Monday).

**Source & more templates:** [onepersoncompany.com](https://onepersoncompany.com/)

---

## How to use this template

1. Duplicate this page each week (name it `Weekly Review — [Week of YYYY-MM-DD]`).
2. Block 30 minutes on your calendar. Same time, every week.
3. Fill it in honestly. The point is signal, not a tidy report.

## Why this matters for a one-person company

Without a boss or a team, nobody else catches the drift. A weekly review is the single highest-leverage habit for a solo founder — it converts a blur of busy days into deliberate direction, and it surfaces the slow leaks (an unsent invoice, a cooling lead) before they cost you.
