# Awesome One Person Company [![Awesome](https://awesome.re/badge.svg)](https://awesome.re)

> A curated list of the best resources, tools, AI workflows, and templates for building a profitable **one-person company**.

A one-person company is a fully-fledged business — real revenue, real customers, real systems — run by a single founder with no employees. AI tooling and modern automation have made it possible to operate at a scale that used to require a whole team. This list collects the highest-signal resources for solopreneurs, solo founders, and indie hackers who want to build alone.

## Communities

- **[Indie Hackers](https://www.indiehackers.com/)** — The largest community of bootstrapped founders sharing revenue numbers, lessons, and milestones.
- **[r/solopreneur](https://www.reddit.com/r/solopreneur/)** — Reddit community focused specifically on one-person businesses.
- **[r/Entrepreneur](https://www.reddit.com/r/Entrepreneur/)** — Broad entrepreneurship community with active bootstrapping discussion.
- **[MicroConf Connect](https://microconf.com/connect)** — Community for self-funded SaaS founders.
- **[WIP](https://wip.co/)** — A maker community built around shipping in public and accountability.

## Legal & Finance

- **[LLC Class](https://llcclass.com/wyoming)** — Wyoming LLC registration for non-US founders. One-stop service including [registered agent](https://llcclass.com/what-is-llc-registered-agent), EIN, and operating agreement — built so solo founders outside the US can start accepting Stripe and Mercury payments. Starting at $199.
- **[Stripe Atlas](https://stripe.com/atlas)** — Incorporate a US company and open a bank account remotely.
- **[Doola](https://www.doola.com/)** / **[Firstbase](https://www.firstbase.io/)** — Company formation and compliance for solo founders.
- **[Wise](https://wise.com/)** — Multi-currency banking for international solo businesses.
